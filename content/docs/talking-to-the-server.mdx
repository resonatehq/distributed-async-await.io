## What this chapter covers

- The HTTP surface and the `{kind, head, data}` envelope
- Creating, reading, and searching promises
- Status codes and what each one means for the client
- Connection lifecycle, transport retries, and backoff
- A minimal client you can build the rest of the SDK on
